Q1. What’s this platform?

This is a crowd-funding online-shopping platform. You can purchase Doggy Coins with less money, and use the Coins to buy different shares of merchandises you like.


Q2. What’s Doggy Coin and how to get it?

Doggy Coin is a virtual currency which is only available in our platform. You can use it to buy different shares of merchandises you like. 1 Coin = 1 rupee. Doggy Coins cannot be trade or withdraw. You can purchase Doggy Coin by tapping our banner. In Tasks, Game and Quiz on our homepage, you can finish tasks to collect free coins, or you can choose to buy more coins in Game or Quiz.

If you do not have enough Coins while purchasing merchandise, you can top up by tapping “OK” on the pop-up window.


Q3. What’s share and how to buy it?

Each merchandise will be divided into different numbers of shares, and each share will be priced at a certain amount of Doggy Coins in accordance with merchandise’s price. (i.e. one share of a 20,000 Coins priced mobile phone with 2,000 shares will be 10 coins)

Just choose the merchandise you want and tap “Join now” to purchase any amount of shares you like. The more shares you buy, the bigger chance you have to win back the merch.


Q4. Rules & Calculation

Each share you buy represents a random lucky number (i.e. a lucky number of a 2000-share merchandise will be randomly generated from 100001 ~ 102001), when all share are sold out, our system will calculate and generate winner’s lucky number.

Winner’s Lucky Number = Remainder + 100001

Remainder = Remainder of (Value A / xxx)

Value A = Sum of last 50 participants' value on our platform

E.g. the purchase time is 11h 23min 34sec 549msec (11:23:34:549), then the value will be 112334549.

And the winner will win back the merch.


Q5. How do I know if I win the merch?

You will receive a message in our APP. After filling out your address and information, you will receive your merch. If you have not fill out the info within 7 days, you will not receive your merch.


Q6. How long does it take to receive the merch?

After we confirmed your address and info, your merch will be delivered within 5 working days. We’ll choose a local express company cooperating with our platform. In case of special circumstances such as natural disasters and other force majeure, we’ll inform you with further notice.


Q7. Can I return or change the merch?

If the merch are found damaged, please keep the original packaging and status of the merch and contact our customer service. If it’s not man-made damage, we will replace them with new ones for you.


Q8. Do I have to pay for the delivery of the merch?

There is no delivery fee.
